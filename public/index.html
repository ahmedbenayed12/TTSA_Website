Welcome & Invitation

Welcome to the 5th TTSA National Congress

The Tunisian Thoracic Surgery Association (TTSA) is delighted to invite you to the 5th edition of its National Congress, held on November 6–7, 2026, in the historic city of Sfax at the prestigious Borj Dhiafa Hotel.

This premier annual event brings together thoracic surgeons, pulmonologists, oncologists, anesthesiologists, and healthcare professionals. This year, our scientific program focuses on innovation, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the sharing of clinical expertise to meet the rapidly evolving technological challenges of our specialty.

A High-Caliber Scientific Program

The scientific committee has curated a comprehensive program featuring dedicated thematic sessions, keynote lectures, and international joint sessions:

Robotic Surgery & Innovation SFCTCV Session

Dive into cutting-edge technical advances with a designated robotic surgery session and a hands-on introductory workshop.

Thymic Pathology

An interdisciplinary approach in partnership with the Tunisian Association of Neurology, covering new guidelines for myasthenia gravis management, the role of lymph node dissection, and minimally invasive approaches.

Endo-bronchial Approaches

A complete overview spanning diagnostic, therapeutic, and palliative techniques led by prominent international experts.

Peri-operative Management APACS Session

In-depth discussions on preventing prolonged post-operative air leaks, pre-operative rehabilitation strategies, and chronic post-operative pain management.

Thoracic Hydatid Cysts & Trauma

Specialized free communication sessions addressing these critical and frequently encountered regional pathologies.

Medical and Allied Health Workshops

Because optimal patient care relies on a cohesive team, the congress offers specialized technical and practical training spaces:

Practical

Introduction to Robotic Thoracic Surgery

Hands-on simulator training and system introduction for surgeons.

Medical

Healthcare Staff Ergonomics

Workplace health, physical strain reduction, and occupational ergonomics in high-stress clinical environments.

Allied Health (Paramedical)

Anesthesia & Access

Targeted sessions on thoracic surgery anesthesia, managing peri-operative anticoagulation, and best practices for permanent vascular/thoracic access.

Research

Posters & Video Sessions

Dynamic presentation areas designed to showcase the research of junior doctors and share complex surgical videos.
