A New Horizon in Healing

The Landmark Triumph of the 1st Pan-Arab Congress of Thoracic Surgery

The inaugural Pan-Arab Congress of Thoracic Surgery was successfully held in Tunis, Tunisia, from November 6–8, 2025, marking a historic milestone for the regional medical community. Hosted at the Diplomatic Academy of Tunis, the event was held in conjunction with the 4th Congress of the Tunisian Society of Thoracic Surgery and received prestigious endorsement from the European Society of Thoracic Surgeons (ESTS).

Oncology Innovations

In-depth sessions focused on the latest multidisciplinary approaches to lung and esophageal cancers.

Minimally Invasive Techniques

Discussions on advancements in VATS (Video-Assisted Thoracoscopic Surgery) and robotic-assisted procedures aimed at improving patient recovery times.

Regional Research

Presentations of clinical data specific to the Arab region, addressing local healthcare challenges and surgical outcomes.
